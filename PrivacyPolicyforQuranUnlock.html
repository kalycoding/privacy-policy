Privacy Policy for Quran Unlock

Last updated: October 27, 2025

Introduction

Quran Unlock ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application Quran Unlock (the "App").

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Daily Quran reading sessions (pages read, time spent)
  • Reading streaks and progress
  • Achievement badges earned
  • Your selected apps to block
  • Your daily reading goals and preferences
  • App settings and configurations

This data remains on your device and is only accessible to you.

Analytics Information

We use Firebase Analytics to understand how users interact with our App. Firebase Analytics may collect:

  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • App usage statistics (features used, time spent in app)
  • General location information (country/region level only)
  • App performance data

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to personally identify you. Firebase Analytics is provided by Google LLC. You can learn more about how Google uses data at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information (unless you create an account in future versions)
  • Your Quran reading content
  • Screenshots or recordings of your device
  • Your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Precise location data
  • Any personally identifiable information

How We Use Information

We use the analytics information to:

  • Improve the App's functionality and user experience
  • Understand which features are most useful
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Make informed decisions about future updates

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Data: All your personal reading data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous analytics data is processed by Firebase Analytics in accordance with Google's privacy standards
  • Security: We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Analytics (Google LLC)

  • Purpose: Anonymous app usage analytics
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

App Permissions

Quran Unlock requires the following permissions to function:

  • Accessibility Service: To detect when blocked apps are opened and display reading reminders
  • Notifications: To send reading reminders and motivational messages
  • Usage Access: To monitor app usage for blocking functionality
  • Boot Complete: To ensure app blocking persists after device restart

These permissions are used solely for the App's core functionality and are not used to collect or share your personal information.

Children's Privacy

Quran Unlock is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Future Features

We may introduce the following features in future versions:

  • User Accounts: Optional accounts for cloud backup of streaks and progress
  • Subscriptions: Premium features available through in-app purchases

If we introduce these features, we will update this Privacy Policy and notify you through the App. You will have the choice to use these optional features.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Delete Your Data: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: You can disable analytics in your device settings or request opt-out instructions by contacting us
  • Access Your Data: All your data is stored locally on your device and accessible only to you

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Data Deletion

Since all your personal data is stored locally on your device:

  • To delete all data: Simply uninstall the App from your device
  • Analytics data: Anonymous analytics data cannot be individually deleted as it is not linked to your identity
